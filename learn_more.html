About CCSIELS

Welcome to the CCS Inventory and Equipment Lending System (CCSIELS)!

This system is designed to streamline the process of borrowing and returning equipment managed by the College of Computing Studies (CCS).

Key Features:
  • Browse Available Equipment: See what items like laptops, projectors, keyboards, mice, cables, and more are available for borrowing.
  • Online Requests: Submit borrow requests directly through the system with details like purpose and duration.
  • Status Tracking: Check the status of your requests (Pending, Approved, Rejected).
  • Borrowing History: View your past and current borrowed items.
  • Automated Reminders: Receive notifications for due dates.
How it Works (The Process):
  1. Log In: Access the system using your official school credentials.
  2. Browse: Find the equipment you need for your academic activities (classes, projects, thesis, etc.).
  3. Request: Fill out the borrowing request form, specifying the item, dates needed, and purpose.
  4. Approval: Wait for the CCS admin/staff to review and approve your request based on availability and policy.
  5. Checkout: Once approved, visit the designated CCS office/lab to pick up the equipment.
  6. Use Responsibly: Take good care of the borrowed item.
  7. Return: Return the equipment on or before the agreed-upon due date to the checkout location.
  8. Check-in: The admin/staff will mark the item as returned in the system.

Our goal is to make accessing necessary resources easier for CCS students and faculty, ensuring equipment is managed efficiently and available when needed for academic success.
