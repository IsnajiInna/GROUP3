Frequently Asked Questions

How do I borrow equipment?

Navigate to "Browse Equipment", find the item you need, and click "Request". Fill out the form and submit.

How long can I borrow items?

The standard borrowing duration is up to 3 days. Check the specific item details or terms if applicable.

What happens if I return late?

Late returns may lead to temporary suspension of borrowing privileges. Please return items on or before the due date.

Who do I contact if I need help?

Please contact the CCS Lab Facilitator or administrator for assistance.
