Frequently Asked Questions

How do I borrow equipment?

Navigate to "Browse Equipment", find the item you need, click "Request", select the specific unit if applicable, fill out the schedule and purpose, agree to the terms, and submit.

How long can I borrow items?

The standard borrowing duration is up to 3 days. This may vary for certain items. Check the terms and conditions or item details.

What happens if I return late?

Late returns may lead to temporary suspension of borrowing privileges. Please return items on or before the due date specified in your borrowed items list or notifications.
